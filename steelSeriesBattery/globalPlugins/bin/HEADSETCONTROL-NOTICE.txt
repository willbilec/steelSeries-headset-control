This add-on bundles headsetcontrol.exe from the HeadsetControl project.

Project:
https://github.com/Sapd/HeadsetControl

Release used:
3.1.0

Purpose:
The executable is used to read supported SteelSeries headset battery status directly on Windows
without depending on SteelSeries GG's local HTTP services.
